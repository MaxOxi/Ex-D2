Focaccia with Caramelized Onion
Craberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetized topped with sweet Caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will became your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the plan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knifeif desired, though
    itâ€™s not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
